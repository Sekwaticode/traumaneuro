Who We Are.

The Trauma Neuro Institute of South Africa (TNISA) is a research-driven institute dedicated to trauma-informed education, counseling, and organizational wellness. We combine social neuroscience, psychology, and global knowledge systems to redefine healing and human development.

  • Founded by: Dr. Letsoalo Montsheng (Dr. Mo) — Social Neuroscientist, Trauma Specialist, and CA-SA Registered Counselor.
  • Recipient of the Young Scientist Award for Best Faculty in Psychology, Dr. Mo’s vision unites rigorous science with restorative practice.

Our Focus Areas.

Trauma & the Nervous System

Exploring how trauma affects brain and body responses, guiding recovery through nervous-system literacy and regulation.

Advancing global understanding of empathy, trauma, and collective healing through research and publication.

Counseling & Healing

Individual, couple, and group therapy that integrates trauma science, cultural psychology, and healing justice.

Corporate Wellness & Neuro-Inclusivity
